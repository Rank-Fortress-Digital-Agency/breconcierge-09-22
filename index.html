A Boston Real Estate Services Company

Founded in 2016 by Caleb L. Butler, Boston Real Estate Concierge has become a one stop shop for all the real estate service needs of the South End community and surrounding neighborhoods. With our five-stars services, we work hard on life’s menial tasks, so you can focus on what you enjoy doing. From running errands to repairs and painting inside the house, to yard cleanup and pressure washing outside the house, providing a second set of hands.

Caleb


Caleb has been in the real estate business for over ten years. With expertise in sales, brokerage and servicing high end estates, he brings a broad set of knowledge to the real estate services industry. For the past five years, he’s been cultivating community ties in the South End in order to provide the highest quality services for our clients and partners. Currently, he’s a dedicated executive, working on the business development strategy and operations division. In his role as BREC’s founder and CEO, Caleb has steered the company into a a one stop shop for all things real estate, from sales and rentals, to interior services such as staging, cleaning, and painting, and exterior services that include snow removal, landscaping and gardening, among others.
